Hi, I'm Steffany Bahamon

Technology Educator & Software Engineer

I'm a Colombian-American technology educator who grew up in Miami and has called Chicago home for the past decade. I've designed and delivered 600+ technical classes, translating complex concepts into engaging learning experiences for audiences ranging from complete beginners to seasoned professionals—in English and Spanish. My work focuses on making technology accessible, building effective learning programs, and using data to understand what's actually working.

When I'm not teaching or building, you'll find me following Formula 1, exploring Chicago's rich history, or planning my next adventure.
