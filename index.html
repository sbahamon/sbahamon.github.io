Hi, I'm Steffany Bahamon

Cyber Defense Educator & Software Engineer

I've delivered 600+ cybersecurity classes, bridging technical and non-technical audiences with bilingual instruction in English and Spanish. My work focuses on making security accessible and building systems that protect what matters.

When I'm not teaching or building, you'll find me following Formula 1, exploring Chicago's rich history, or planning my next adventure.
